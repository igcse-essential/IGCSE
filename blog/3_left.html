Essential Resources

Now, a large portion of students struggles at the very end of the marathon. That is, they do not know which resources they should use in order to practice their past papers. Be aware of the fact that the majority of websites in the Internet claim to offer past papers but their mark schemes are all 'fake.' That is, you will not be able to understand how IGCSE marks your answers and will never truly know if the answers are actually accurate.

Other resources that students often find lacking are notes and essential advice. That is why I took the initiative to dedicate my time for this website. Here is a list of websites I found immensely helpful when it comes to nailing my IGCSE's.

Explanations:

ㅤㅤ1) Khan Academy

ㅤㅤ2) Sally Weatherly (IGCSE)

ㅤㅤ3) Crash Course

Notes and Past Papers:

ㅤㅤ1) Z Notes

ㅤㅤ2) XtremePapers

ㅤㅤ3) PapaCambridge

Revision per Topic:

ㅤㅤ1) SavemyExams

ㅤㅤ2) PhysicsandMathTutor

ㅤㅤ3) SmashingScience

ㅤㅤ4) MathmadeEasy

The last 2 are pretty challenging and should definitely be in your bucket list of websites to try! If you are someone who organizes a schedule for revision and love to do lists, I highly recommend Todoist. I personally use it all the time and helps me keep track of all the essential things I have to accomplish.
