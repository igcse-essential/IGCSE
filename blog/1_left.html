Nail Your IGCSEs

Welcome to this website. Through this website, you would be able to find concise and detailed notes which may help you in your revision. I will also be regularly posting videos(tutorials) on questions I that I find particularly challenging.

Right of the bat, I can 100% assure you that there is no absolutely no need to stress about your IGCSE's. Now, that does not mean to just ignore it and cram everything last minute. Simply said, you do not have to adhere to a strict schedule where you revise an hour a day for each subject. In fact, I find that method unhelpful at all. The curriculum is designed to not be very vigorous. As long as you understand your concepts and once in a while, let's us say, every weekend, you take a little time of for revision(after all, 2 years is a long time and you may forget what you have learn earlier), you will be fine.

Now, after personally completing the curriculum with a high distinction, I have personally found study tips that are incredibly helpful. I have also discovered certain exam mistakes that are repeated every single year which you should avoid at all cost to not waste marks.

With that said, I hope you find this website helpful and if you have ANY questions at all, feel free to contact me at girvin.djapardi@sisschools.org.
